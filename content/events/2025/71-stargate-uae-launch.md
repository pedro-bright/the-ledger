## Summary

OpenAI, G42, Oracle, NVIDIA, and SoftBank launched Stargate UAE — a 1 gigawatt AI campus in Abu Dhabi positioned as the first international extension of the US Stargate project and the anchor of a 5 gigawatt US-UAE AI Campus.

## What Happened

On May 22, 2025, a consortium including OpenAI, Abu Dhabi's G42, Oracle, NVIDIA, and SoftBank announced Stargate UAE at a ceremony coinciding with President Trump's state visit to the UAE. The flagship site — a 1 gigawatt AI campus near Abu Dhabi — would be the first facility in a planned 5 gigawatt US-UAE AI Campus, described as the largest AI infrastructure project outside the United States.

The first operational phase targeted 200 megawatts of capacity online by Q3 2026. G42, the Abu Dhabi technology conglomerate with sovereign backing, provided the local partner and site. The US-UAE AI Acceleration Partnership framing embedded Stargate UAE within a bilateral diplomatic framework in which UAE pledged reciprocal investment in US AI infrastructure and research, partially addressing concerns from US legislators about technology transfer to Gulf states with ties to China. Oracle would provide cloud infrastructure; NVIDIA would supply GPU clusters; SoftBank would provide capital and strategic coordination.

## Why It Matters

Stargate UAE was simultaneously a commercial deal, a diplomatic instrument, and a test of the export control framework. Commercially, it represented OpenAI's first major international infrastructure play and a significant expansion of the Stargate brand beyond US soil. Diplomatically, it bound the UAE more closely into the US AI orbit — a deliberate strategic objective given Huawei's established presence in UAE data centers and China's existing technology relationships in the Gulf. As an export control matter, the GPU shipments required for a 1 gigawatt campus implied tens of billions of dollars in chips subject to BIS licensing — demonstrating that sovereign AI deals operated at the intersection of commercial, diplomatic, and security considerations simultaneously.
